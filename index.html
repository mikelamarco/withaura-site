FAQ

Common questions.

Does this work with my existing booking system?
+
Your booking system stays exactly as it is. Aura AI connects on top of it — we integrate with Vagaro, Mindbody, Boulevard, Jane App, and Square. Your clients book the same way they always have. We set the integration up during your onboarding call.
Will my clients be annoyed by the automated texts?
+
The opposite happens. Our open rate is 92% and response rate is 64% because messages only go to clients who haven't visited in 30+ days, and every message references their name and their last actual service. It reads like a personal note, not a blast. Unsubscribe rates average under 2%. Clients tell owners they appreciate the outreach.
Where does the deposit money go?
+
Straight to your Stripe account — Aura AI never holds your money. Deposits go directly from the client's card to your bank. We're just the mechanism that collects it. You can see every transaction in your Stripe dashboard in real time.
How long does setup take?
+
One call, typically 45 minutes. We handle your service list, deposit policy, message templates, staff access, and client import. You don't need to know anything technical — most owners describe it as surprisingly fast. Everything is live before the call ends.
Is there a contract? Can I cancel?
+
No contracts. Month to month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We keep clients because the system delivers results — not because they're locked in. Your first 30 days are completely free, no credit card required.
What if I don't have an online booking system yet?
+
Even easier to set up. We'll configure Square Appointments (free) and connect it to Aura AI in the same call. You leave with a complete booking and automation stack running — often in under an hour total.
How is this different from Podium?
+
Podium is a broad customer communications platform. It handles messaging across channels, but it doesn't run revenue recovery automatically — you still need staff to manage the inbox, respond to leads, and manually push membership offers. Aura AI is built for one thing: recovering and growing revenue at med spas, with no ongoing work from your team. No-show deposits collect automatically. Lapsed client texts go out every morning without anyone touching a keyboard. Membership offers fire to the right clients at the right time. Podium also runs $1,000–$2,000+/month for a comparable feature set. Founding members get Aura AI at $497/month, locked forever.
Is my clients' payment data secure?
+
Yes. Aura AI never stores card numbers. All payment processing runs through Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified — the highest level of payment security available. Your clients' card data goes directly to Stripe. We never see it, never touch it, never store it.
What if I don't see results?
